## FILING INSTRUCTIONS

### To File This Document:

1. **Choose Your State:** File in your state of residence OR Delaware (business-friendly laws)

2. **Online Filing:**
   - Delaware: https://icis.corp.delaware.gov/
   - [Your State Secretary of State website]

3. **Filing Fees:**
   - Delaware LLC: ~$90
   - Most states: $50-$500

4. **Required Information:**
   - Company name (verify availability first)
   - Registered agent name and address
   - Organizer information
   - Purpose of business

5. **After Filing:**
   - Receive Certificate of Formation
   - Obtain EIN from IRS
   - Open business bank account
   - Create Operating Agreement

## STATE-SPECIFIC NOTES

### If Filing in Delaware:
- File Certificate of Formation (not Articles of Organization)
- Annual franchise tax: ~$300
- No state income tax on LLC income if operating outside Delaware

### If Filing in Your Home State:
- May need to also file in Delaware as a foreign LLC if incorporated there
- Check local business license requirements
- Review state-specific LLC formation requirements
