## üî¢ PHASE 2: EIN (EMPLOYER IDENTIFICATION NUMBER)

### Timeline: Immediate | Cost: FREE

#### Application Process
1. Go to: https://www.irs.gov/businesses/small-businesses-self-employed/apply-for-an-employer-identification-number-ein-online
2. Select "Limited Liability Company" as entity type
3. Select "Started new business" as reason
4. Enter LLC information
5. Receive EIN immediately upon completion

#### Information Needed
- [ ] Legal name of LLC
- [ ] State where LLC was formed
- [ ] Date LLC was formed
- [ ] Responsible party name & SSN
- [ ] Business address
- [ ] Business phone number

---

## üî¢ PHASE 3: D-U-N-S NUMBER

### Timeline: 1-14 days | Cost: FREE

> **Required for both Google Play (organization) and Apple Developer Program**

#### Application Process
1. Go to: https://www.dnb.com/duns-number/get-a-duns.html
2. Click "Get your D-U-N-S Number"
3. Search to confirm business doesn't already have one
4. Complete application with business details
5. Wait for email confirmation (typically 1-14 business days)

#### Information Needed
- [ ] Legal business name
- [ ] Business address
- [ ] Business phone number
- [ ] Number of employees
- [ ] Year business started
- [ ] Primary contact information

#### Expedited Options
- Apple provides expedited D-U-N-S lookup through their enrollment process
- Can take 2-5 days through Apple vs 14 days direct

## ü§ñ PHASE 5: GOOGLE PLAY DEVELOPER ACCOUNT

### Timeline: 1-2 days | Cost: $25 (one-time)

#### Account Registration
1. Go to: https://play.google.com/console
2. Sign in with Google Account
3. Pay $25 registration fee
4. Choose account type: **Organization** (not Individual)
5. Complete identity verification
6. Submit for review

#### Requirements Checklist
- [ ] Google Account (create business-specific account)
- [ ] Valid payment method (credit/debit card)
- [ ] D-U-N-S Number
- [ ] Business name matching LLC
- [ ] Business address
- [ ] Business phone number
- [ ] Business website URL

#### Identity Verification
- Photo ID verification through Google
- May require video call
- Processing: 24-48 hours

## üçé PHASE 6: APPLE DEVELOPER PROGRAM

### Timeline: 1-3 weeks | Cost: $99/year

#### Enrollment Process
1. Get D-U-N-S Number (if not already obtained)
2. Go to: https://developer.apple.com/programs/enroll/
3. Sign in with Apple ID (create business-specific account)
4. Select "Organization" enrollment
5. Enter D-U-N-S Number
6. Complete verification process
7. Pay $99 annual fee
8. Wait for Apple review

#### Requirements Checklist
- [ ] Apple ID (business-specific email recommended)
- [ ] D-U-N-S Number (must be active/valid)
- [ ] Legal entity name matching D-U-N-S exactly
- [ ] Legal binding authority (or Letter of Authorization)
- [ ] Business website with:
  - [ ] Privacy Policy page
  - [ ] Contact information
  - [ ] Support page/email
- [ ] Valid phone number for verification call

#### Verification Process
Apple may require one of:
- Video call verification
- Notarized document
- Letter from legal/accounting professional

#### Common Rejection Reasons
- D-U-N-S info doesn't match enrollment info
- Website doesn't show privacy policy
- Business name mismatch
- Unable to verify legal authority
