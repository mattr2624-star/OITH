---

## What is an EIN?

An **Employer Identification Number (EIN)** is a unique nine-digit number assigned by the IRS to identify your business for tax purposes. It's like a Social Security Number for your company.

---

## Why You Need an EIN

1. **Open a Business Bank Account** - Required by all banks
2. **File Business Tax Returns** - Even as a single-member LLC
3. **Hire Employees** - Required for payroll taxes
4. **Apply for Business Licenses** - Many require an EIN
5. **Establish Business Credit** - Separates personal and business credit
6. **Work with Vendors** - Many require W-9 with EIN
