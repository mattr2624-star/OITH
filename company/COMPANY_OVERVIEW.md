## Company Description

OITH (One In The Hand) is a premium dating application designed to facilitate meaningful connections through a unique "one match at a time" model. The platform addresses the fundamental problems of traditional dating apps—low response rates, superficial swiping, and power imbalances—by creating a focused, intentional dating experience.

### Core Value Proposition
- **One Match at a Time:** Users receive a single, AI-calibrated match daily
- **Paid Platform:** $10/month subscription ensures serious daters only
- **Date-Focused:** Built-in tools to move from chat to in-person dates
- **Balanced Power Dynamics:** Equal investment from all users
