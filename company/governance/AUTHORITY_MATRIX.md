## Delegation of Authority

### Current Delegations
*None - All authority retained by CEO*

### Future Delegation Framework

When employees or contractors are engaged, authority may be delegated as follows:

| Role | May Be Delegated |
|------|------------------|
| Developer/Contractor | Technical decisions within project scope |
| Marketing Contractor | Campaign execution within approved budget |
| Bookkeeper | Routine financial recording (no spending authority) |
| Customer Support | User issue resolution within policy guidelines |

**All delegations require:**
1. Written authorization from Matthew Ross
2. Defined scope and limits
3. Regular reporting requirements
4. Can be revoked at any time
