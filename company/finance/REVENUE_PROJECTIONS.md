# Revenue Projections & Market Analysis
## One In The Hand, LLC

---

## Executive Summary

OITH operates on a simple subscription model: **$10/month** for all users. This document outlines revenue potential based on market size analysis and user growth projections.

---

## Pricing Model

| Tier | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Monthly** | $10/month | Full access, all features |
| **Annual** | $96/year ($8/month) | 20% discount |

**Net Revenue per User (after payment processing):**
- Monthly: $10.00 - $0.59 (Stripe fees) = **$9.41**
- Annual: $96.00 - $3.09 (Stripe fees) = **$92.91** ($7.74/month)
